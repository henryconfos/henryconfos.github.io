How To Play


Try and guess each of the four words in 4 tries. Click a box then enter a letter to guess.

  • If the letter is in ANY of the words it will show up as green

  • If a letter is in ANY of the 4 words it will show up as yellow.

  • If the letter is not in any of the words it will show up as grey and a life will be removed.
